Pancakes with oats

Ingredients:


- 2½ dl milk with a maximum of 0.7% fat
- ½ hp. yeast (25 g)
- 1 egg
- 1 tbsp. fat
- 1 tsp. sugar
- 1 tsp. salt
- 250 g whole wheat flour
- 250 g flour (approx. 4 dl)


For cooking

- 1 l sell
- 2 tsp. salt


Brushing

- 1 egg


Preparation


Soak the oatmeal in the milk for approx. 15 min. Whisk flour, eggs, sugar, baking soda, cardamom and salt in. Add the fat and whisk the dough completely.
Distribute barely ½ dl of dough on a frying pan and cook for 2-3 minutes. Turn over the pancake and fry on the other side.
If the frying pan has Teflon coating, no extra fat is needed. Bake a total of 14 small pancakes.


Serving


Serve brunch pancakes with oats for brunch or for a snack.


Alternative


Brunch pancakes with blueberries 100 g of blueberries, preferably frozen, are turned into the dough before baking the pancakes.
1 cup of milk can be replaced by 1 cup of light pilsner.
